Vaishnavi Gujjula

Associate Professor, PES University, Bengaluru

04S6, BE Block

PES University

RR Campus, BSK Stage III, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560085

Dr. Vaishnavi Gujjula is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AIML), PES University, Bengaluru.

She began her academic journey as an Assistant Professor in the Department of CSE (AIML), PES University in July 2024.

She has done a Post-Doctoral Fellowship under the guidance of Prof. Dipanjan Gope in the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering, IISc, Bengaluru from July 2023 to July 2024. She worked on fast solvers for computational electromagnetics and scientific machine learning.

She pursued her PhD under the guidance of Dr. Sivaram Ambikasaran in the Department of Mathematics and the Department of Computational & Data Sciences at IISc Bengaluru. Later she continued her PhD at the Department of Mathematics, IIT Madras, and successfully defended her PhD thesis in 2023. Her thesis is in the field of Computational Mathematics with applications to N-body problems in physics and machine learning.

From 2014-2016 she worked as a Deputy Engineer in the Fire Control Systems (FCS) unit of Naval Systems SBU, at Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), Bengaluru.

She holds a B.Tech degree in Electronics & Communication Engineering from KL University, Andhra Pradesh.
